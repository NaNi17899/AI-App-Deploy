Docs & FAQ

How do I deploy without JavaScript?

All interactions on this site are CSS-only. Your app can still use JS—this demo simply shows the front-end can be minimal.

Can I customize the theme?

Yes. Adjust CSS custom properties in the :root block to change colors, radii, spacing, and typography.

Is this production-ready?

Yes. It’s responsive, accessible, and optimized. Replace placeholder copy and assets with your own.

How do I connect my repository?

Simply authorize your Git provider (GitHub, GitLab, Bitbucket) and we'll automatically detect your project settings.

What AI frameworks are supported?

We support all major frameworks including TensorFlow, PyTorch, Hugging Face, and OpenAI APIs.
